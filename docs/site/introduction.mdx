[MemoBase](https://memobase.io) is a fast and scalable storage to mangage your user memory.
It maintains a dynamic profile for each user, and comtinuously updates over time.
You can intergate MemoBase in your AI to provide a more personalized experience to your users.

MemoBase is now on active development, and we are looking for early adopters to become our design partners!
