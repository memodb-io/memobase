[Memobase](https://memobase.io) is a fast and scalable backend to manage your user profile from unstructured data.
It maintains a dynamic profile for each user, and continuously updates over time.
You can integrate Memobase in your AI to provide a more personalized experience to your users.

Memobase is now on active development, and we are looking for early adopters to become our design partners!
[Contact us](mailto:contact@memobase.io) if you are interested
